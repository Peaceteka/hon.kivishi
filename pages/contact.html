Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with the foundation?

+

You can volunteer by filling out our volunteer application form or contacting us directly. We have various opportunities in sports coaching, education support, medical camps, and community development projects.

How do I apply for educational bursaries?

+

Bursary application forms are available at our ward office and during specific announcement periods. Applicants must be residents of Manda-Shivanga Ward and demonstrate financial need. Watch our news section for application deadlines.

Can I request assistance for housing?

+

Housing assistance is prioritized for the most vulnerable members of our community. Applications are reviewed based on need criteria including disability, age, and family circumstances. Visit our office to submit a housing assistance request.

How can I donate to support your programs?

+

Donations can be made through our office, mobile money transfer, or bank account. We have specific funds for education, healthcare, housing, and general community development. All donations are transparently used for intended purposes.

When do you organize medical camps?

+

Medical camps are organized quarterly or as funding allows. We announce dates through our website, social media, and community mobilizers. Follow our news section for upcoming medical camp schedules.

How can my organization partner with you?

+

We welcome partnerships with NGOs, corporate organizations, government agencies, and academic institutions. Send us a detailed proposal about your organization and proposed collaboration area. We'll review and get back to you within a week.

Ready to Connect?

Whether you want to volunteer, partner, or learn more about our work, we're here to help. Reach out today and be part of our community transformation journey.
