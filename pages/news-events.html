Manda-Shivanga Super Cup 2026 Kicks Off

The annual youth football tournament has begun, bringing together teams from across the ward for an exciting competition that promotes talent development and community unity.

10 February 2026 Social Welfare

New Housing Project Launched for Vulnerable Families

Construction has begun on three new houses for families in need, continuing our commitment to community welfare and dignified living conditions.

The housing project, funded through community contributions and foundation resources, will provide safe and decent shelter for three vulnerable families including widows and elderly persons. Each house will feature two bedrooms, a living room, kitchen, and modern sanitation facilities...

5 February 2026 Health

Free Medical Camp Serves 500+ Residents

Our recent medical outreach provided essential healthcare services to over 500 community members, including general check-ups, treatments, and health education.

The medical camp, held at Manda-Shivanga Primary School, brought together healthcare professionals from various facilities who volunteered their time and expertise. Services included blood pressure screening, diabetes testing, malaria testing, and general consultations...

1 February 2026 Education

Bursary Awards Ceremony Celebrates Student Success

Over 50 students received educational bursaries from the Kivishi Foundation, enabling them to continue their studies without financial constraints.

The annual bursary ceremony, held at the ward office, celebrated academic excellence and provided financial support to deserving students from vulnerable backgrounds. The bursaries cover school fees, examination fees, and learning materials for both secondary and tertiary students...

28 January 2026 Infrastructure

Road Rehabilitation Project Improves Community Access

Partnership with local sugar factory leads to improvement of 5km of rural roads, enhancing connectivity and market access for farmers.

The road rehabilitation project, a collaboration between the foundation and West Kenya Sugar Company, has transformed the main access roads in the ward. The improved roads now make it easier for farmers to transport their produce to market and for students to commute to school...
