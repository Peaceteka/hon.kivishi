Servant Leadership in Action: Transforming Manda-Shivanga Ward Together

Hon. David Iraya Kivishi is dedicated to empowering our youth, developing our communities, and delivering quality public service. Through strategic initiatives in education, healthcare, sports, and infrastructure, we're building a brighter future for every resident of Manda-Shivanga Ward.
